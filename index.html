Step into a space where every step is crafted to help you grow, thrive and shine with true confidence and purpose.
You are a masterpiece, uniquely hand picked and crafted by the universe and here, your potential is honoured. The Crafted One is an exclusive space where transformation takes center stage. From accent refinement and public speaking to psychological counseling, mindfulness, and beyond; my endeavour is to elevate your skills, refine your mindset and nurture holistic well-being.

As a psychologist, HR professional and a life coach, I've had the previlege of guiding over 2,000 individuals to become more confident, and empowered in their personal and professional lives.

With an MBA from Mahatma Gandhi University and an MS in psychology from the Higher School of Economics, Moscow, I have a strong academic background in understanding different types of people and businesses. My professional experience is diverse. Initially I started as a sales executive and went on to work as an HR officer at Bharat Heavy Electricals for six years until I secured a fully funded scholarship to do my MS in Russia. In Russia, I worked as a part time English trainer and also obtained a Cambridge certification (CELTA) in teaching English as a foreign language. After returning to India in 2021, I continued teaching English online and began my journey with Amrita University as a soft skills trainer. I find teaching and mentoring a very fulfilling experience and I am constantly trying to learn new things which I can share with my students.

My approach is centered on creating a safe, open space for my students to express themselves, discover their strengths and unlock their dormant potential. To me, learning is not just about gaining skills. It's also about shaping a more refined, confident and compassionate version of oneself.

At The Crafted One, I'm dedicated to helping you grow holistically by crafting your journey into one of lasting success and fulfillment.

Services that I offer

  • Spoken English Sessions

    Upgrade your communication to a new level of elegance and confidence with my Spoken English sessions. My tailored approach focuses on fluency, vocabulary and refining your pronunciation. With me you will be able to speak with clarity, poise and impact.

  • Public Speaking Sessions

    Enhance your stage presence and captivate any audience with my exclusive public speaking workshops. From crafting powerful messages to cultivating the art of confident delivery, I will help you speak with authority, charm and charisma.   • IELTS/TOEFL Preparation

    Achieve your dream score with my customised IELTS and TOEFL preparation sessions. From enhancing your language skills to mastering test strategies, I provide personalized coaching to help you excel in every section of these international exams.

    There could be episodes in our lives when we feel overwhelmed by challenges and hardships. If you are looking for a safe space to discuss your concerns, I provide an empathetic, non judgemental and supportive space to help you find clarity, resilience, and balance. Together, we'll work to navigate life's complexities with confidence and grace, paving the way for a healthier and more fulfilling life.

  • Personality Development

    Uncover the most refined version of yourself with my transformative personality development programs. By enhancing your communication, boosting your confidence and cultivating a positive attitude, I will help you shape a compelling presence.
